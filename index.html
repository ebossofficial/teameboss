TEAMEBOSS

TEAMEBOSS is a Volunteer Network (Non-profit) formed by youths with like minds bringing together Volunteers across Nigeria.
We offer a seamless registration process ensuring all registered attendees are checked-in faster via Check-ins applications. Also, we offer Social Media PR, running the event hashtags, posting pictures from your event with valuable Content for more engagements.

We became a Strategic partner to THE AFRICA FINTECH SUMMIT LAGOS 2018 Organized by Dedalus Global (A Washignton DC based organization) and LAGOS FINTECH WEEK 2019 where my Team was the sole volunteer partner in ensuring the check-ins of registered attendees was properly done and faster using e-platform for that cause. We have also volunteered for (social, technology, hackathons) events and have received Awards for Volunteering and collaborations.
